Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I schedule social media posts?

+

Scheduling posts saves you time and helps you maintain consistency online. You can plan your content in advance, post at optimal times even when you're busy, and ensure your audience always sees fresh content. It's especially useful during weekends, evenings, or when you're away from your phone.

Do you support M-Pesa payments?

+

Yes! FURSA supports M-Pesa payments for all subscription plans. Simply choose M-Pesa at checkout and follow the prompts to complete your payment securely.

Can I schedule posts to Instagram?

+

Yes, FURSA supports scheduling to Instagram feeds. Connect your Instagram Business account and schedule posts, images, and captions to go live automatically.

Does FURSA work with slow internet?

+

Yes! FURSA is optimized for Kenya's internet conditions. Once you schedule a post, it's saved in the cloud and will be published automatically - even if you're offline or experiencing slow connectivity at the scheduled time.

Can I try FURSA for free?

+

Absolutely! We offer a free plan with 5 scheduled posts per month and 1 social account. You can also start a free trial of our Basic or Pro plans to test all features before committing.

Which social media platforms does FURSA support?

+

FURSA currently supports Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter/X. We're working on adding LinkedIn, WhatsApp Business, and TikTok in future updates.
